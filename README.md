# ⚖️ AI Contract Risk Analyzer

> Upload any contract → Get instant risk analysis in plain English or your regional language.

---

## What Is This?

A free AI-powered tool that reads your contracts and tells you — in plain language — what's risky, what to watch out for, and what to change. Built specifically for Indian users, keeping Indian Contract Act 1872 in mind.

No legal jargon. No confusion. Just clear, practical answers.

---

## Features

- 📄 Upload PDF or TXT contracts
- ✂️ Auto clause-by-clause analysis
- 🔴🟡🟢 Risk color coding — HIGH RISK · WARNING · SAFE
- 🔥 Consequence engine — real ₹ estimates, legal risks, practical scenarios
- ✏️ Safer rewrite suggestions for every risky clause
- 🌐 10 languages — English, Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam + more
- 👤 Role-based analysis — Student, Freelancer, Tenant, Employee, Business Owner, Startup Founder, NRI, Senior Citizen, and more
- 📥 Download full PDF report
- 🔒 Privacy-first — documents never stored or shared

---

## Risk Color Scheme

| Color | Level | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 Red | HIGH RISK | Dangerous clause — negotiate or reject |
| 🟡 Orange | WARNING | Needs attention — ask questions |
| 🟢 Green | SAFE | Generally fair clause |

---

## How To Use

1. Select your **role** and preferred **language** from the sidebar
2. Upload a **PDF or TXT** contract (or use the sample for demo)
3. Click **Analyze Contract**
4. Browse results in the risk tabs
5. Download your **PDF report**
